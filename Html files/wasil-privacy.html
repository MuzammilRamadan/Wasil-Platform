Privacy Policy

How WASIL collects, uses, and protects your personal and health-related data.

Back to Landing Page
Last updated: April 2026

1 Introduction

WASIL ("we," "our," or "the Platform") is an electronic health platform designed to support epidemic response and mobile clinic management in Sudan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and role (Community Member, Organization, or MOH Admin).
  • Health Reports: Disease symptoms, case descriptions, and location data submitted when reporting cases.
  • Location Data: Area or address information provided when requesting services or reporting outbreaks.
  • Service Requests: Type of service requested, contact information, and any related medical information.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the platform, pages visited, and features used.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for technical support.

3 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for public health purposes:

  • To facilitate epidemic response and coordinate mobile clinic deployments.
  • To connect community members with appropriate health services.
  • To generate anonymized outbreak statistics and heatmaps for health authorities.
  • To process and manage service requests (vaccination, ambulance, medical consultations).
  • To send critical health alerts and notifications to affected communities.
  • To improve the platform's functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with public health reporting requirements.

4 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Ministry of Health (MOH): For epidemic monitoring and official health response coordination.
  • Registered Health Organizations: Only the information necessary to fulfill your service requests.
  • Supabase: Our secure backend database and authentication provider, subject to their privacy policy.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by Sudanese law or court order.

All shared data is subject to confidentiality agreements and is used only for public health emergency response purposes.

5 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Role-based access control — only authorized personnel can access sensitive data.
  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • Secure authentication via Supabase with email verification.
  • Regular security audits and monitoring for unauthorized access.
  • Data minimization — we only collect what is necessary for health response.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: wasil-platform@gmail.com

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Anonymized health data may be retained longer for public health research and epidemic trend analysis. Upon account deletion, personal identifiers are removed within 30 days.

8 Children's Privacy

WASIL is designed for adults and health professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 16 years of age. Health reports submitted on behalf of children must be done by a parent or guardian.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email or in-platform notification. Continued use of WASIL after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10 Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us:

Email: wasil-platform@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
