الأسئلة الشائعة

How do I register as a Community Member?

Click "Get Started" on the landing page, select "Community Member" as your role, and complete the sign-up form with your email and password. Verification is required before accessing the platform.

How can my organization apply to deploy mobile clinics?

Register as an "Organization" on the platform. Once your account is approved by MOH admins, you can access the clinic assignment dashboard to submit deployment requests for affected areas.

Is my health data kept confidential?

Yes. All personal health data is encrypted and only accessible to authorized health personnel. Case reports are aggregated and anonymized for epidemiological analysis. Privacy Policy

The platform is available in Arabic?

Yes! WASIL supports full Arabic and English localization. You can toggle between languages using the language button in the top navigation bar across the platform.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your registered email. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact us at wasil-platform@gmail.com.
