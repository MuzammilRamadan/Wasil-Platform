Health Instructions

Wash Hands Frequently

Use soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially before eating and after using the restroom.

Drink Clean Water

Always boil or filter water before drinking. Avoid untreated or stagnant water sources.

Report Symptoms Early

If you experience fever, diarrhea, or vomiting, report to the nearest clinic immediately.

Stay Vaccinated

Ensure you and your family are up to date with recommended vaccinations.
